    It's a Wonderful Life

    One of my favorite films of all time is Frank Capra's It's a Wonderful Life (1946). I deeply admire this film because of its heartfelt message, its stellar acting, and its raw portrayal of a struggling man's emotions. Here you can find one of the most visceral and emotional scenes.

    Silence

    One my favorite films is Martin Scorcese's Silence (2016). I find this film fascinating for the themes it presents, its meditative and thoughful pace, and lastly its profoundness.

    Spider-Man 2

    One of my favorite films is Sam Raimi's Spider-Man 2 (2004). I enjoy this film because of its charm and humor, a compelling look at what it means to be a superhero, and its humanity.
